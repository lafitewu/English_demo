You Can Always Communicate Better.

Studio Marani has been created in 1990 following the desire of Maurizio Marani (its CEO and
Creative director) to continue the value course he began in Mc Cann Erickson, being the agency
Senior art director for more than 15 years. At Mc Cann Ericsson Maurizio Marani has been
working for clients such as Gillette, Nescafe, Algida, William Lawson’s, General Motors,
Lagostina, La Rinascente and more others.
Today studioMarani is a tiny communication agency with huge clients, this following the idea of
a cutting edge communication realized like a sartorial suit: with passion, profound know-how
and exclusively tailor-made, with the deep belief that’s there’s always a way to communicate
better.
From 2011 with the arrival of Anna Scardovelli - creative director and copywriter, screenwriter
and author- the agency has been furthermore empowered. Studio Marani tends to create long
term relationships with clients: to make an example, Maurizio Marani is following Radio Deejay
communication since its very beginning (almost 30 years ago). We can say the same thing even
for Gruppo Espresso Radio, Radio Capital and m20 Radio.
Studio Marani offers to its clients the quality of a big agency, in a more fluid and dynamic
dimension, with a fixed team of professionals and a huge network of collaborators.
